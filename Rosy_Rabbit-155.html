Rosy Rabbit

For a flair, fix in chafing dish

ingredients

  • 1 can condensed tomato soup
  • 2 cups American cheese, grated
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 egg whites, stiff-beaten
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten

directions

1. Heat: Heat soup in top of double boiler; add cheese and heat until melted, stirring constantly.
2. Add: Add mustard and Worcestershire sauce to beaten egg yolks; slowly stir in tomato mixture.
3. Fold: Fold in beaten egg whites
4. Heat: Heat thoroughly over hot water, stirring occasionally.
5. Serve: Serve on hot toast or crackers. Garnish with chopped parsley and frizzled dried beef, crisp bacon, or ham.
