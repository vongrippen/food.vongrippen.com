Golden Fruit Salads

  • homepage
  • previous
  • next

ingredients

  • 2 cups canned fruit cocktail
  • 1 ⅓ cups fruit cocktail syrup
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 envelope Knox Gelatine
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • ⅓ cup orange juice
  • salad greens

directions

1. Drain: Drain fruit cocktail
2. Heat: Heat the syrup with sugar. Soften Knox Gelatine in lemon juice; dissolve in hot liquid.
3. Add: Add orange juice; cool until syrupy. Stir in fruit cocktail.
4. Pour: Pour into small moulds; chill until firm.
