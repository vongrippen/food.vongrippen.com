Tomato-soup French Dressing

Sharp-flavored to complement a crisp tossed salad

ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can condensed tomato soup
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup salad oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion

directions

Combine: Combine ingredients in bottle or jar; cover and shake thoroughly.
