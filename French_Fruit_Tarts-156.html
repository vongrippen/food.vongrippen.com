French Fruit Tarts

  • homepage
  • previous
  • next

ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups canned fruit cocktail
  • 1 3oz pkg cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fruit cocktail syrup
  • 2 tablespoons mint jelly
  • 6 3-inch tart shells, baked

directions

1. Drain: Drain fruit cocktail
2. Soften: Soften cream cheese with syrup. Add lightly beaten jelly; beat until fluffy.
3. Spread: Spread cream cheese mixture in bottom of tart shells; fill each shell with fruit cocktail.
