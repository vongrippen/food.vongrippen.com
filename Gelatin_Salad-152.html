Gelatin Salad

  • homepage
  • previous
  • next

ingredients

  • 2 packages cherry gelatin
  • 1 small can crushed pineapple
  • ½ can black cherries
  • 1 small package cream cheese
  • 1 can cola
  • 1 cup nuts

directions

1. Drain: Drain pineapple and cherries, saving juices
2. Heat: Heat the combined juices and use to mix the gelatin
3. Mix: Mix a small portion of hot juices with cream cheese until it is a thin paste.
4. Add: Add the cola to gelatin mix and stir in the cream cheese mixture, this may require an electric mixer to smooth out the lumps.
5. Stir: Stir in cherries, pineapple, and nuts.
6. Pour: Pour into a gelatin pan or mould, and refrigerate until set.
